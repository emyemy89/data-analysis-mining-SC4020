Lisboa | anthem                          = Marcha a Lisboa }} '''Lisbon''' is the [[capital (city)|capital city]] of [[Portugal]]. It is the largest city of Portugal. The [[city]] has a [[population]] of about half million people. In Lisbon's urban area live around 2.8 million people, being the 10th-most populous urban area in the [[European Union]].  Lisbon is placed on the right bank (western) of the [[Tagus]] River, near the outfall. It has a pleasant climate and has about 220 days of [[sun]]shine each year. There are many beautiful [[beach]]es close to the city. There are also many [[seafood]] [[restaurant|restaurants]], [[history|historical]] sites and monuments.
