It is in the southeast, north of [[Czech Republic]]. To the east of Saxony is [[Poland]]. The biggest city is [[Leipzig]]. The capital is called [[Dresden]]. It was founded in 1990.  == Geography == Saxony is the [[border]] of [[Germany]] to the two countries [[Poland]] and [[Czech Republic]]. In the north of Saxony is [[Brandenburg]], another German state. Saxony's west border is to [[Saxony-Anhalt]], [[Thuringia]] and [[Bavaria]].
