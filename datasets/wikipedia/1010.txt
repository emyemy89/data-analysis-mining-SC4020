<title>List of German football teams</title><text>These are the [[Germany|German]] [[football (soccer)|football]] teams and the [[league]]s they played in for the 2013/14 [[season]] (1st to third league). The highest German league is the [[Bundesliga]] (First Division). Below it is the second ''Bundesliga'' (Second Division). The Third Division is called 3. Liga. The Fourth Division, called ''[[Regionalliga]]'' is split into a northern, a southern and a western part.
