Most species have two [[sex]]es {{ndash}} male and [[female]]. [[Human]] males are [[man|men]] or [[boy]]s; human females are [[woman|women]] or [[girl]]s.   The two sexes have different [[sex organ|sexual organs]], and different [[secondary sex characteristic]]s. They also often have different biological functions. The female gives [[birth]] to [[child]]ren with the [[semen]] provided by the male.
