Video game consoles usually [[wikt:connect|connect]] to a [[television]].   Special [[Game controller|controller]]s are attached to the console to control the game.  Some video game consoles are [[handheld]]. They can be carried around and have their own [[screen]]s. The [[Nintendo DS]] and [[PlayStation Portable]] are examples of handheld consoles.
