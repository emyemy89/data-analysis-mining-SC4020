<title>Tongue-twister</title><text>A '''tongue-twister''' is a [[phrase]] that is hard to [[speech|say]]. They often use [[alliteration]] and [[homophone]]s. He would chuck as many wood as a woodchuck chucks if a woodchuck could chuck wood. * She sells sea-shells on the sea-shore.
