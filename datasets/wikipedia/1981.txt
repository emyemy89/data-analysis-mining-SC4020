It is famous for its [[Information technology]] [[industry]] and so it is called the '''[[Silicon Valley]] of India'''. Today as a large and growing city, Bangalore has many of the most well-recognized colleges and research institutions in India. {{commons}} Bangalore is more famous for its climatic conditions.
