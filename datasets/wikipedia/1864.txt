He was born [[August 15]], [[1983]] in [[Coventry]], England. He is a [[goalkeeper]]. He plays for the [[England|English]] football team [[Swindon Town F.C.|Swindon Town]].  In the 2003/04 season, McCormick played when Romain Larrieu could not.  Larrieu injured his [[knee]]. McCormick played instead.  Plymouth Argyle beat Stockport County on [[24 January]] [[2004]]. The [[score]] was 2-0. In this match McCormick set a new club record of seven ''clean sheets'' in a row. A clean sheet is a game where the other side gets no goals.  McCormick was voted the most promising player of the 2003/04 season by Plymouth Argyle fans.  On 7 June [[2008]]. McCormick was arrested on suspicion for killing two people by dangerous driving.
