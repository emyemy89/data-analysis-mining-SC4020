<title>Interim</title><text>'''Interim''' means "in between" or "transitional" (moving from one time or place to another), or "temporary."  Some common "interim" items:  * An [[interim report]] is a report on the how some work is going. * An [[interim document]] is a piece of writing that is not finished. * An [[interim official]] is a person who is doing a job temporarily. This can be in between two other people having the job, or when the normal person is temporarily unable to do it.   {{disambig}}</text>
