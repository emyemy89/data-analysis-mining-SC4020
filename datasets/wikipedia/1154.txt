<title>Brussels</title><text>[[Image:Luc Viatour Hotel de ville Bruxelles 1.JPG|thumb|right|200px|City hall at the [[Grote Markt (Brussels)|Grote Markt]]]] [[Image:Greater Coat of Arms of Brussels.svg|right|90px]] [[Image:Drapeau ville de Bruxelles.png|right|200px]] '''Brussels''' ({{lang-fr|Bruxelles}}, {{lang-nl|Brussel}}, {{lang-de|Brssel}}) is the [[Capital (city)|capital city]] of [[Belgium]] and the [[European Union]].   In 2007, 145,917 people lived there.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://statbel.fgov.be/downloads/pop200701com.xls |title=Werkelijke bevolking per gemeente op 1 januari 2007 / Population de droit par commune au 1 janvier 2007 (.xls) |access-date=2008-07-15 |archive-date=2008-11-23 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081123144002/http://statbel.fgov.be/downloads/pop200701com.xls |url-status=dead }}</ref> But the area around it, known as the [[Brussels-Capital Region]], had 1,031,215 people (which makes it the largest city area in Belgium).  The people of Brussels speak mainly [[French language|French]] and [[Flemish]] (a type of [[Dutch language|Dutch]]). There are lots of other languages spoken as well, because the European Union offices are there and because many thousands of [[immigrant]]s from all over the world live there.  The city is at 50 50 North, 04 21 East.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://statbel.fgov.be/pub/d1/p120_nl.xls |title=Geografische cordinaten van de gemeenten (.xls) |access-date=2008-07-15 |archive-date=2004-07-23 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20040723190930/http://statbel.fgov.be/pub/d1/p120_nl.xls |url-status=dead }}</ref> It has an [[oceanic climate]] (''Cfb'' in the [[Kppen climate classification]]).   Thirty-one people [[2016 Brussels bombings|were killed]] in a [[terrorist]] attack on 22 March 2016.  ==Influence== Many popular European [[comics]] came from Brussels, such as ([[Tintin]], [[The Smurfs]], [[Snorks]], [[Lucky Luke]]).   It is also well known for a style of building known as ''[[Art Nouveau]]''.  The vegetables [[Brussels sprout]]s are named after the city, and Brussels is also famous for its [[waffle]]s and its [[chocolate]]s.
