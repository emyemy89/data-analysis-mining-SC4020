<title>19th century</title><text>{{centurybox |19}}  The '''19th century''' was the [[century]] from [[1801]] to [[1900]].  Most of this century is normally called the [[Victorian era|Victorian period]] because [[Queen Victoria]] ruled the [[United Kingdom]]. [[File:Princess Victoria and Dash by George Hayter.jpg|thumb|[[Queen Victoria]]]] The [[Industrial Revolution]] started in this century in most [[west]]ern [[country|countries]].
