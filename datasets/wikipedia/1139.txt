It is in the northern United States, in the state of [[Minnesota]]. It is a [[tributary]] of the [[Mississippi River]]. It is about 534 km long.  The source of the river is a lake in western Minnesota. The lake is called Big Stone Lake. The lake is on the border between the state of Minnesota and the state of [[South Dakota]]. From its source, the Minnesota flows southeast. At [[Mankato, Minnesota|Mankato]], it turns northeast. It joins the Mississippi just south of the cities of [[Saint Paul, Minnesota|Saint Paul]] and [[Minneapolis, Minnesota|Minneapolis]].
