<title>Aberystwyth Town F.C.</title><text>'''Aberystwyth Town Football Club''' are a [[Wales|Welsh]] [[football (soccer)]] team.  The club was started in [[1884]]. They play at the Park Avenue stadium in [[Aberystwyth]]. They play in the [[Welsh League]].
