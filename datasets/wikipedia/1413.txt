<title>Healthy diet</title><text>[[File:USDA Food Pyramid.gif|thumb|right|325px|{{Center|[[United States Department of Agriculture]] [[food pyramid]] (click to enlarge)}}]] A '''healthy diet''' or '''balanced diet''' is a [[diet (nutrition)|diet]] (what you eat) that contains the right amounts of all the [[food group]]s. It includes [[fruit]], [[vegetable]]s, [[grain]]s, [[dairy products]], and [[protein]]. It does not include too much or too little of any kind of food.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://gethealthy.eliv.info/cgi-bin/index.pl/diet.html |title=Natural, Organic Healthy Living & Diet |access-date=2007-12-11 |archive-date=2018-02-11 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180211145025/http://gethealthy.eliv.info/cgi-bin/index.pl/diet.html |url-status=dead }}</ref> Eating wrong amounts of a food group, whether it be too much or too little, is called an "unhealthy diet" or an "imbalanced diet". A healthy diet is one that includes more foods that come from [[plant]]s and fewer [[convenience food]]s.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.berkeleywellness.com/healthy-eating/food/slideshow/14-keys-healthy-diet |title=14 Keys to a Healthy Diet |author= |website=University of California Berkeley Wellness |publisher= based on the major food groups that includes vegetables, whole grains, low-[[fat]] [[dairy product]]s.<ref name=Mayo/> It should also contain lean [[protein]] such as [[bean]]s, legumes, [[nut lifestyle and (healthy) preferences.<ref name=Mayo/> * Drink plenty of water.<ref name=PCFSN>{{cite web |url=http://www.fitness.gov/eat-healthy/how-to-eat-healthy/ |title=How to Eat Healthy |author= [[sugar]].<ref name=PCFSN/> * Read the [[Nutrition facts label|food labels]] to see what is in the foods.<ref name=PCFSN/> For [[example]], labels usually show what foods are lower in [[Table
