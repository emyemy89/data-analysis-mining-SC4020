<title>Suffix</title><text>A '''suffix''' is a few letters put at the end of a word to change its meaning. <ref>"suffix." The American Heritage Dictionary of the English Language, Fourth Edition. Houghton Mifflin Company, 2004. 12 Aug. 2007. <Dictionary.com http://dictionary.reference.com/browse/suffix>.</ref> It is a type of [[affix]] mostly used to show Inflection  Verb examples: * -ed * -er * -ing * -s * -ing * walk * walked * walker * walking * walks  [[Adjective]] examples: * -er * -est  * easy * easier * easiest  Compare to [[prefix]]. Prefixes are letters put at the beginning of a word to change its meaning.  == References == {{reflist}}   {{stub}}  [[Category:Grammar]]  [[fr:Affixe#Place des affixes]] [[nn:Affiks#Suffiks]]</text>
