<title>Frying</title><text>To '''fry''' [[food]] is to [[cook]] it in hot [[butter]] or [[vegetable oil]] or other [[fat]]. We can fry food in a small amount of fat in a [[frying pan|pan]] or in a lot of oil in a [[pot]]. Some [[restaurant]]s use [[deep frying]] to fry a large amount of food.  {{food-stub}}  [[Category:Cooking methods]]</text>
