<title>Accrington Stanley F.C.</title><text>'''Accrington Stanley Football Club''' is an [[England|English]] [[football (soccer)|football]] club.  The club will play in [[Football League Two|League Two]] (the English fourth division) in the 2015/16 season.  They were re-[[wikt:founder|founded]] in 1968 after going bankrupt (having no money) in 1962. They were first founded in 1921. They are thought of as hard working but having no chance of success. Their [[nickname]] is Stanley.
