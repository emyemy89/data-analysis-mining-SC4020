The club plays in the [[English Premier League|Premier League]]. Their current [[stadium]] is The Tottenham Hotspur Stadium in north [[London]]. The captain of Tottenham Hotspur is Hugo Lloris and vice-captain is [[Harry Kane]]. [[Jos Mourinho]] is the manager.  The club's most common nickname is Spurs. Other nicknames include The Lilywhites and occasionally "Yid Army".  Famous Tottenham Hotspur players include [[Pat Jennings]], [[Paul Gascoigne]], [[Glenn Hoddle]], [[Dave Mackay]], [[Gary Lineker]], [[Danny Blanchflower]], [[Teddy Sheringham]] and [[Jimmy Greaves]]. They have many rivals in London, but their most famous rivals are [[Arsenal F.C.|Arsenal]]. Tottenham were the first winners of the UEFA Cup tournament, and the first team in the 20th century to achieve the league and cup double.
