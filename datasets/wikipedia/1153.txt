<title>20th century</title><text>{{centurybox|20}} The '''20th century''' began on January 1, 1901 and ended on December 31, 2000. It was the century that saw two [[world wars]], the [[Soviet Union]] rise and fall, the coming of the [[telephone]], [[sound recording]], [[film]] and [[television]], [[aeroplane|airplane]]s, [[atomic bomb|atomic weapons]], [[genetics]] and [[DNA]], [[computers]] and [[electronics]] in general. [[Science]] and [[industrialisation|industrialization]] spread; [[medicine]] became more scientific.
