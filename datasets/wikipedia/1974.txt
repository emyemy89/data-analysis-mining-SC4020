<title>Box</title><text>[[File:Box.agr.jpg|thumb|An empty cardboard box.]] A '''box''' is a [[container]] used to put things in. It is mainly a [[cuboid]] ([[Square (geometry)|square]]) shape, but it can be other shapes too. Boxes are usually made out of [[cardboard]],<ref>{{cite web|title=Cardboard Box|url=https://thegaylordboxexchange.com/cardboard-walls/|publisher=The Box Exchange|accessdate=14 September 2017}}</ref> [[wood]], [[plastic]] or [[metal]]. Many boxes are made of cardboard, and a lot of them are made. They are primarily used for packaging commercial goods or storing goods and materials. They can be made from bent or wrinkled [[cardboard]]. In their most obvious life stage, they are popularly used as a cheap material to hold a range of projects, among them being science experiments, children's toys, costumes and other things.  A '''box''' also means a [[rectangle]], ''e.g. userbox''. But rectangles are mainly referred to as rectangles, the term box is the outline, or [[perimeter]] of the rectangle.  ==References== {{commonscat|Boxes}} {{reflist}}   {{tech-stub}}  [[Category:Basic English 850 words]] [[Category:Containers]]</text>
