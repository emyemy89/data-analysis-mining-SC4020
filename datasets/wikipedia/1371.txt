<title>Palette knife</title><text>[[File:Palette knife.jpg|right|thumb|Palette knives]] A '''palette knife''' is not actually a [[knife]]. It has a very flexible [[steel]] blade and no sharpened cutting edge. It is a [[tool]].  ==Uses== A palette knife is used for mixing [[paint]] colors.
